ABOUT

EndCraft is a free-to-play Minecraft server, featuring a PVP-focused server for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. EndCraft supports many gamemodes, ranging from practice, to minigames, duels, and even survival. We support Minecraft: Bedrock Edition versions 1.4.2 through 1.16 on almost every device. You can play on EndCraft with a device that runs on Windows 10, Linux and macOS (using this guide), Android, iOS, Windows Mobile, and an Xbox One, PS4, Nintendo Switch, Amazon Fire Stick, Amazon Fire TV, and Gear VR Headset.

GAMEMODES

EndCraft currently supports the following gamemodes:

NODEBUFF

In NoDebuff, players are put in an arena with Potions of Healing, Potions of Speed, Ender Perals, and Enchanted Diamond Gear! You can fight other players to the death or just practice your aim!

SUMO

In Sumo, two players fight to knock eachother off a floating platform in the sky. The last player on the platform wins!

COMBO

Combo is a gamemode all about clicking the fastest. With Enchanted Golden Apples, Enchanted Diamond Armor, and no hit cooldown, it's a great gamemode for practicing your speed! Try to get long, consistent bursts of hits on other players to be the best!

BUILDUHC

In BuildUHC, players fight just like in a normal PVP gamemode, but they can build! Will you win with pure skill or use strategy in this gamemode?

FIST

In fist, players fistfight eachother to the death! Like NoDebuff, this gamemode is a great way to practice your aim!

UPCOMING

At EndCraft, we're always trying to improve the server to make player expreinces better. For that reason, we always have some new and upcoming gamemodes in the works, like the ones we're developing right now, King Of The Hill, UHC Meetup, and Skywars!
