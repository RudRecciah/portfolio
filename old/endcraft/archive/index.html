ABOUT

EndCraft is a free-to-play Minecraft server, featuring a PVP-focused server for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. EndCraft supports many gamemodes, ranging from practice, to minigames, duels, and even survival. You can learn more about EndCraft here.

JOIN

You can join EndCraft using any device which supports Minecraft: Bedrock Edition using the IP play.endcraft.org and the port 19132. If you are on Xbox, you must be logged into an Xbox Live Gold account. Is Minecraft: Bedrock Edition not supported on your device? Click here to learn how to install Minecraft: Bedrock Edition on operating systems that don't support it.

RANKS

Although EndCraft is free to play, we do have microtransactions for people who want to take their experience to the next level. EndCraft ranks support a variety of cosmetics like gadgets, suits, capes, and more. Learn more about each rank and buy a rank here.

STAFF

Staff are an integeral part of any server. They moderate, develop, host, and do a plethera of other things. Here at EndCraft, we value staff and allow anyone who thinks they are up for the task to apply for a position. Learn more about staff here.

DISCORD

Need support? Looking to chat with others in our community? Thinking about applying for staff? Want to appeal a ban? Join our discord server!
